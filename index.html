üéÉ What is Crimson Eye?

Crimson Eye is Phasmophobia's annual Halloween event, featuring the ominous Blood Moon weather across select maps. This year's event runs for three weeks and includes both individual and community goals with automatic reward claiming.

Key Improvements for 2025:

  • More Maps: Event expanded from 4 to 7 maps with daily rotation
  • Auto-Claim Rewards: No need to interact with totems - rewards are automatically claimed upon login
  • Trophy Upgrades: Complete Personal Goals to upgrade your Crimson Eye Trophy
  • Better Balance: Improved progression system based on feedback from 2024

Action Points Earned
Correctly identify the ghost 10 points + 1 per difficulty multiplier

Important: Contracts with a 0x difficulty multiplier reward no points. Play on higher difficulties for maximum contribution!

üí° Tips for Surviving the Blood Moon

  • Bring Sanity Pills: Your sanity drains faster, so stock up on sanity restoration items
  • Know Your Hiding Spots: With faster ghost speeds, you need to be ready to hide quickly
  • Use Smudge Sticks Wisely: Save them for critical moments during hunts
  • Communicate Clearly: Teamwork is essential when facing increased difficulty
  • Take Photos: Don't forget to photograph totems for extra points
  • Complete Objectives: Each optional objective adds 3 points - they're worth the effort!
  • Check Daily Rotation: Plan your play sessions around which maps are active each day
  • Play on Higher Difficulties: The difficulty multiplier increases your point rewards

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

When does the event end?

The Crimson Eye event runs until October 31, 2025 at approximately 11:59 PM local server time.

Do I need to claim rewards manually?

No! This year, all rewards are auto-claimed. Once a goal is completed, you'll receive the rewards automatically upon your next login during the event window.

Can I play solo?

Yes! You can contribute to community goals whether playing solo or with a team.

What if I miss a day?

No problem! The event runs for three weeks, and maps rotate daily, so you'll have multiple chances to play each map.

Will Blood Moon weather be available after the event?

Yes! Blood Moon was permanently added to the game as a random weather type after the 2024 event.

I already have the 2024 trophy. What can I earn?

You can still earn the new Child of the Cult ID Card & Badge set, plus trophy upgrades through Personal Goals.
